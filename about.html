Our Story

At Kahawa Lounge, we celebrate coffee that is rooted in Kenya’s highlands. Each bean begins its journey in Nyeri and Kiambu, grown by smallholder cooperatives and nurtured through every roast.

We believe in a roasting philosophy that keeps the origin intact: bright, fragrant, and soulful. Our cafe is a place where families and friends gather, inhale warm espresso notes, and enjoy food inspired byAfrican flavors.

From the hills of Mt. Kenya to the heart of Kakamega Town, our goal is to make each cup an authentic experience of craft, community, and kindness.
